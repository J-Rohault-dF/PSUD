The United States of America is a federal republic consisting of 50 states, 1 district and 5 territories. It was established in 1776 by taking its independence from the British Empire.

Canada is a constitutional monarchy in the commonwealth, consisting of 10 provinces and 3 territories. It was established in 1867 by taking its independence from the British Empire.

The European Union is non-sovereign union, consisting of 28 member states. It was established in 1993 by replacing the European Economic Community (established in 1958).
